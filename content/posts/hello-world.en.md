## Why Blog

Knowledge is the beginning of action; action is the completion of knowledge.

The idea behind this blog aligns with Hugo's philosophy — less tooling, more content. In an era where AI evolves daily, watching without practicing is not enough. A place to document and solidify real practice is needed.

## Why Hugo

After some research, I chose Hugo with the PaperMod theme:

- **Hugo**: Blazing fast builds, zero runtime dependencies, great CJK support
- **PaperMod**: Clean and minimal, dark mode, SEO-friendly, active community
- **Cloudflare Pages**: Global CDN, generous free tier

Technology shouldn't be a barrier to writing. Less tooling, more content.

## What's Next

- **AI Practice**: Agent exploration, prompt engineering, LLM applications, technical reflections
- **Tech Tinkering**: Toolchains, productivity, interesting projects
- **Life Notes**: Reading notes, parenting and education, daily reflections

Unity of knowledge and action. This is where it begins.
